What type of report are you filing?

👤 Payroll Employee Report

For your direct employees — people on your payroll for whom you withhold federal social security taxes. Required if you have 100+ payroll employees with at least 1 in California.

🏢 Labor Contractor Employee Report

For workers hired through a staffing agency or labor contractor. Required if you have 100+ labor contractor employees with at least 1 in California. Filed separately from payroll report.
